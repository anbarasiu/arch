About Anbarasi U

Anbarasi U (also known as Anbu) is a software engineer, poet, and writer based in Frankfurt, Germany. She creates at the intersection of technology and creativity, maintaining multiple projects that explore different facets of expression and thought.

Projects

  • Lit (lit.anbuu.in): A collection of poetry and literary verses exploring themes of identity, technology, philosophy, and human experience. Features original poems, translations, and creative writing.
  • Fluid (fluid.anbuu.in): Technical writing and software engineering insights. Covers web development, programming concepts, technology trends, and innovative approaches to building digital products.
  • Gush (gush.anbuu.in): Personal musings and reflections on life, culture, philosophy, and travel. A space for thoughts that don't fit neatly into technical or poetic categories.
  • Fable (fable.anbuu.in): Interactive storytelling and narrative experiments that blend technology with traditional storytelling methods. Creative fiction and innovative narrative formats.
